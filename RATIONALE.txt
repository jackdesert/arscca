Q: Why are slow outliers grouped (conformed) with the next fastest driver?
A: To make the histogram labels easier to read. (Less dense)

Q: Why does arscca.org fetch /calendar/plain via ajax instead of via iframe?
A: Setting innerHTML on a div on arscca.org allows the content to flow naturally,
   and the content renders full height.

Q: Why was typescript added?
A: Jack wanted experience with typescript

Q: Why are ESM modules used instead of AMD?
A: They are the wave of the future.

Q: Why is Browserify used instead of a competing technology like webpack?
A: Jack tried webpack first, but the default settings totally mangled the code
   such that it was nearly impossible to read. (And it used eval)
